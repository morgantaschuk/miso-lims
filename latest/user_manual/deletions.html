24. Deletions

24.1. Deletions List

To get to the Deletions list page, click “Deletions” in the Tracking list in the menu on the top left side of the screen. This list includes basic information about items that have been deleted by MISO users, as well as information about when the item was deleted.

This table is view-only; deleted items cannot be updated or restored.
